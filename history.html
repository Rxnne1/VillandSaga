THORFINN THORSSON

From revenge… to peace

The warrior's son

Thorfinn Thorsson is the protagonist of Vinland Saga. Born in Iceland, he is the son of Thors, a legendary Viking warrior who abandoned war to live in peace. From a young age, Thorfinn admired the strength and honor of warriors, dreaming of adventures and battles.

However, his fate changes when he witnesses his father's death in an ambush. That moment shatters his innocence and ignites within him an absolute desire for revenge. From then on, his life revolves around combat and hatred.

The path of revenge

Thorfinn joins Askeladd's group the man responsible for his father's death with a single purpose, to defeat him in a fair duel. He grows up on the battlefield, honing his skills and becoming a lethal fighter.

For years he lives only to fight. He seeks neither glory nor riches, only the chance for revenge. But when he finally loses his purpose, he is left empty and adrift.

“You have no enemies. Nobody has enemies.”
